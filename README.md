Project Overview

The Electrical Vehicle (EV) Data Analysis Dashboard provides insights into the adoption, distribution, and characteristics of electric vehicles across years, counties, cities, models, and vehicle types.
It supports decision-making related to sustainable transportation and clean mobility planning.

**Objectives:**

Analyze EV growth trends over model years

Understand regional and county-level EV distribution

Compare CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility

Identify popular EV models and types

Support infrastructure and policy planning
